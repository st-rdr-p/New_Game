SEQ main_greeting
  NPC: OldSage
    "Welcome, brave adventurer." (voice_sage_welcome.mp3, 2.5)
    "I am the keeper of the Sacred Crystal."
    "Dark forces have been searching for it..."
    
  CHOICE
    [I'll help you] -> SEQUENCE: accept_quest
    [Why me?] -> SEQUENCE: question
    [Not interested] -> SEQUENCE: reject

SEQ question
  NPC: OldSage
    "You seem capable, perhaps even destined." (voice_sage_destiny.mp3, 2.3)
    "The ancient prophecies spoke of one like you."
    
  CHOICE
    [I'm convinced] -> SEQUENCE: accept_quest
    [Still no] -> SEQUENCE: reject
    [Tell me more] -> SEQUENCE: lore_dump

SEQ lore_dump
  NPC: OldSage
    "Legend speaks of a time of darkness..." (voice_sage_legend.mp3, 4.0)
    "When shadow consumed the lands."
    "But a hero emerged, wielding light itself."
    "Now, history may repeat. Will you be that hero?"
    
  CHOICE
    [Yes, I will] -> SEQUENCE: accept_quest
    [No way] -> SEQUENCE: reject

SEQ accept_quest
  NPC: OldSage
    "Excellent!" (voice_sage_excited.mp3, 1.5)
    "The Crystal rests in the Mountain Peak."
    "Beware - many creatures guard it."
    "Bring it back safely, and the realm shall be saved." (voice_sage_final.mp3, 3.0)

SEQ quest_update
  NPC: OldSage
    "Have you found the Crystal?" (voice_sage_check.mp3, 2.0)
    
  CHOICE
    [Almost there] -> SEQUENCE: encourage
    [Still searching] -> SEQUENCE: hint
    [Found it!] -> SEQUENCE: crystal_obtained

SEQ encourage
  NPC: OldSage
    "You are doing well, I can feel it!" (voice_sage_encouragement.mp3, 2.5)
    "Do not give up now!"

SEQ hint
  NPC: OldSage
    "Seek the highest peak. That is where it lies." (voice_sage_hint.mp3, 2.5)
    "The creature guarding it is weak to fire."

SEQ crystal_obtained
  NPC: OldSage
    "YES! You have done it!" (voice_sage_joy.mp3, 2.0)
    "The realm is saved. You are a true hero." (voice_sage_thanks.mp3, 2.5)

SEQ reject
  NPC: OldSage
    "So be it. Perhaps when you are braver..." (voice_sage_disappointed.mp3, 2.5)
    "Should you change your mind, I will be here."
