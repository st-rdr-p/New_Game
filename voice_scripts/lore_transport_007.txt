NARRATIVE SIGNIFICANCE:

This is the EMOTIONAL CLIMAX before the final boss fight.

The protagonist learns:
1. They've been carrying someone's soul (in the ring)
2. They may have killed them without knowing
3. The Entity has been manipulating them all along
4. Everything they did was predetermined in the Entity's "rehearsal"

The abrupt ending forces the player to:
- Complete the realization themselves ("That was... her?")
- Carry this uncertainty into the final confrontation
- Experience the same disorientation and horror the protagonist feels
- The question becomes: Is the Entity telling the truth? Or is it lying?

GAMEPLAY CONTEXT:
- Ring item changes appearance (darker, dimmer red)
- Ring gains tooltip: "Someone else's soul"
- Player enters the hardest fight while emotionally compromised
- This is why the Entity was "rehearsing" — it was setting up this exact moment
- The protagonist has been a pawn the entire time

TONE & PACING:
- This entry is SHORT deliberately
- Fast, brutal, devastating
- No time to process
- Just like the narrative: BAM — realization — CUT
- The brevity is part of the power

THE SILENCE:
- The silence after "That was—" is NOT a technical issue
- It's the player sitting with the horror
- It's the protagonist frozen in realization
- It's the moment before everything changes
